100 DAYS OF UI

A daily design project over the next 100 days, where I will focus mainly on design patterns, micro-interactions, and wireframes; and post my results on Twitter and LinkedIn.

Since the daily prompts serve mainly as inspiration, I will take the artistic liberty of completing them in the order I choose and may even "stretch" a prompt in a different direction. This way, I can focus on my main goal, which is to have fun experimenting with as many design patterns and micro-interactions as I can in 100 days.

If you feel inspired to start this challenge yourself, please visit Daily UI to sign up for their daily prompts. Now, onward!

Day #001: Sign Up

DESIGN PATTERN /// (1) Input Feedback


GOAL /// To give the user real-time feedback regarding the status of their inputs and actions. This technique reduces a user's confusion and/or frustration when completing a tedious task. View Prototype


Day #002: Credit Card Checkout

DESIGN PATTERN /// (1) Breadcrumbs / (2) Tunneling


GOAL /// (1) To show users the location of their webpage within the entire website, and (2) to guide them through a process without any distractions. These techniques give the user a sense of control over their navigation and helps them focus on the task at hand. View Prototype
